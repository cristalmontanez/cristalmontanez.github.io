In Memory of My Journey About Me

In Loving Memory of:

Jose Nicolas Montanez

1951 -2024

José Nicolás Montañez Useche, born in San Cristóbal, Venezuela, was a dedicated electrical engineer and attorney, as well as a loving father to his daughters, Ericka and Cristal. Known for his kind heart and strong faith, José was a devout Catholic who attended Mass twice daily and offered his time to help at church. His compassion extended to all, whether through caring for people in need, taking in stray cats, or looking after his mother until his final days. José believed in the power of kindness and the importance of community. He was proud of his daughters’ accomplishments and lived a life of humility and grace. We honor his memory and the legacy of love, kindness, and faith he leaves behind.
